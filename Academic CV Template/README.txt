Academic CV Template

Author: Dubasi Pavan Kumar

Description:
This LaTeX template is designed for creating professional academic CVs. It provides a clean, organized structure for showcasing your academic achievements, research experience, publications, and more. The template is highly customizable, allowing you to tailor it to your specific needs and academic field.

Key features include:
- Clean and professional design suitable for various academic disciplines
- Customizable sections for personal information, education, research experience, publications, awards, etc.
- Easy-to-use LaTeX commands for adding and modifying content
- Responsive layout that adapts to content length
- Options for different fonts and color schemes
- Built-in support for including hyperlinks and social media profiles

This template is ideal for graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and faculty members looking to create a compelling academic CV. It's designed to highlight your scholarly achievements and present your academic profile in a clear, organized manner.

License: Creative Commons CC BY 4.0

Usage Instructions:
1. Open the template in Overleaf or your preferred LaTeX editor.
2. Replace the placeholder text in each section with your own information.
3. Compile the document to generate your personalized academic CV.

Customization:
- Font changes can be made by modifying the \headerfont command in the preamble.
- Colors for links and section headings can be adjusted in the color definition section.
- Sections can be added, removed, or reordered to suit your needs.

For detailed customization instructions, please refer to the comments within the LaTeX file.
